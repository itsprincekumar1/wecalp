About Us

A Nurturing Space for Every Child

WeCalp is a therapy and development center dedicated to special needs children. We focus on growth, overcoming deficiencies, and building essential life skills — all within a gentle, supportive environment.

Our programs serve individuals from as young as 3 months up to 28 years, ensuring continuous support through every stage of development.
